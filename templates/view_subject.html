The hum of focused energy fills the physics lab as Mr. Chen demonstrates the principles of electromagnetism. Wires snake across the table, connecting batteries, coils, and a small, spinning motor. He doesn't just show; he invites students to participate, to experiment, to witness the elegant dance of electrons and magnetic fields. It's a testament to the profound and lasting impact of a teacher who ignites the flame of scientific curiosity.
